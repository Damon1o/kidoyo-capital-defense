Where It Started

Wellington C. Mepham High School has always been a place that pushes students to think beyond the textbook. It was in these halls that our team came together, united by a shared love of technology and a desire to build something that actually matters.

As part of the BMCHSD community, we entered the Capital Defense hackathon with one goal: to design, build, and defend a solution that makes a real impact. What started as an after-school idea became a full-stack project backed by months of research and late nights.
