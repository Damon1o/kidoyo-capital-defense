Our Story

From a small kitchen in Lyon to shelves across Europe.

It Started With a Kernel

In 2018, founder Amélie Bertrand was on a mission: to bring the art of French cuisine to the world of popcorn. Tired of bland, uninspired snacks, she set up a small popcorn kitchen in Lyon and started experimenting with bold flavours inspired by French cooking.

What started as weekend batches for friends quickly became something bigger. The flavours were undeniably good — inventive, delicious, and distinctly French. Word spread fast.
