Our School

Wellington C. Mepham High School is a proud part of the Bellmore-Merrick Central High School District (BMCHSD), located in Bellmore, New York. Known for its strong academic programs, vibrant extracurriculars, and a community-first culture, Mepham has long been a launchpad for student success.

From athletics to the arts, and now competitive coding — Mepham students consistently rise to the challenge. Our Capital Defense hackathon team is no exception, representing the school on a regional stage against the best young hackers on Long Island.
