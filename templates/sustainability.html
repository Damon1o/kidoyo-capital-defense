Sustainability

Doing right by the planet, one kernel at a time.

We believe great popcorn shouldn't cost the earth — literally. From our sourcing to our packaging, we make choices that are better for the planet.

🌾

Responsible Sourcing

All our corn is grown by certified sustainable farmers in France who use regenerative practices to protect soil health and biodiversity.

♻️

Recycled Packaging

Our bags are made from 80% recycled material. We're working towards 100% compostable packaging by 2026.

🚛

Low-Carbon Shipping

We offset 100% of our delivery emissions and partner with carbon-neutral couriers wherever possible.

🤝

Community First

1% of every sale goes to French farming communities and rural development programmes.
